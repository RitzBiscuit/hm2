Welcome

Drop of Prana is a non-profit organisation spread across 6 continents and 100+ countries. We make sure that people in high risk areas of natural disasters are able to stay safe in disaster situations as well as providing them with something to rely on. We collect donations from millions of people around the world, and what we recieve from all the loving people across the globe and use it to help others in danger.

To prepare for the eventuality of a Natural Disaster, visit our What to do page and learn how to stay safe in the event of any natural disaster. If you or your family are situated near a high risk disaster area and wish to sign up to our free monthly services then check out the Contact Page. You will have access to emergency funds, shelters, necessities.

If you wish to donate to the cause the go to this Donate page and fill the form in order to help a family in need.
